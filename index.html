SHA-1 HASH FUNCTION ALGORITHM

Abstract

SHA - Secure Hash Algorithm was published by the US National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) in 1992 as PUB FIPS 180 and was revised in 1995 and named SHA-1.


Message length must be less than 264 bits.
